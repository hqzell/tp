## Overview

**RACE (Resident Administration & Contact Explorer)** is a desktop application designed to help Resident Assistants (RAs) efficiently manage resident information in student housing.

During orientation periods, RAs often need to onboard **40+ residents quickly**, while handling large amounts of personal information across spreadsheets, forms, and messaging apps. These fragmented workflows are slow, error-prone, and stressful.

RACE provides a **single, structured system** to manage resident details safely and efficiently.

---

## Target User

RACE is designed for Resident Assistants like **Felix**, a Year 3 computing student and RA at Acacia College.

Felix enjoys interacting with residents and organising events, but during orientation he becomes overwhelmed by the messy process of onboarding residents and managing their information across different tools.

RACE helps Felix keep track of all residents under his care in one organised system.

---

## Key Features

RACE focuses on **efficient resident information management** rather than communication.

The system allows RAs to:

- Add resident records with key details
- Edit resident information when updates are required
- Delete residents who have moved out
- Search for residents quickly using names or partial information
- View all residents under their care in one organised interface
- Store sensitive resident information privately

Each resident record can include:

- Name
- Unit number
- Student ID
- Contact number
- Telegram handle
- School email
- Dietary requirements

RACE acts as an **information navigator**, not a communication platform.

---

## Example Use Cases

### Orientation Onboarding

An RA needs to register dozens of new residents quickly during orientation.
RACE allows rapid entry of structured resident data so onboarding can be completed efficiently.

### Emergency Situations

If a fire alarm goes off, the RA can quickly check which residents are supposed to be in the rooms on their level.

### Mid-Semester Updates

Residents may change rooms or update their contact details.
The RA can easily search for a resident and edit their information.

### Semester Turnover

At the end of the semester, some residents move out while new ones arrive.
RACE helps RAs clean up or archive records without losing important information.

---

## Why RACE?

Current RA workflows often involve:

- Multiple spreadsheets
- Messaging platforms
- Manual notes
- Disorganised resident lists

RACE replaces these scattered tools with **one consistent and reliable system**, reducing mental load and making resident management simpler and faster.

## Technology

RACE is built using:

- **Java**
- **JavaFX**
- **Object-Oriented Programming principles**

The project is based on the **AddressBook Level 3 architecture**, adapted for managing resident information.
